Maple salmon

Description

The recipe is for Maple Glazed Salmon. It's a baked salmon dish that features a delicious glaze made with maple syrup, soy sauce, Dijon mustard, olive oil, garlic powder, salt and pepper. To prepare the dish, you will need to preheat the oven to 400째F (200째C) and line a baking sheet or baking dish with parchment paper. Then, you will place the salmon fillets on the prepared baking sheet or dish, and pour the maple glaze over them. The salmon is then baked in the oven for 12-15 minutes or until the salmon is cooked through and flaky. Once cooked, the salmon is garnished with sliced green onions for added flavor and presentation. The resulting dish is a flavorful and tender salmon fillet with a sweet and savory maple glaze. It's perfect for a healthy and delicious dinner option, and can be served with a variety of sides such as roasted vegetables, rice, or salad.

Ingredients

  • 4 salmon fillets
  • 1/4 cup maple syrup
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp Dijon mustard
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Sliced green onions for garnish (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 400째F (200째C).
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the maple syrup, soy sauce, Dijon mustard, olive oil, garlic powder, salt and pepper until well combined.
  3. Place the salmon fillets on a lined baking sheet or in a baking dish.
  4. Pour the maple glaze over the salmon fillets, making sure they are evenly coated.
  5. Bake the salmon in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes, or until the salmon is cooked through and flaky.
  6. Once cooked, remove the salmon from the oven and garnish with sliced green onions (if desired).
